Checkins Flow Analysis Tool

In this project I was working with Fourqsquare checkins data. A checkin is a tuple of user, venue and timestamp, that a user leaves when he wants to show his presence at a certain place. We can use the histories of checkins of users that visit places of interest to better understand different patterns of behavior that exist in these places.

The visualisation was focused on two aspects: general information about the cluster and transitions between categories of venues. The first aspect, the general information, shows you an overview of a selected cluster: most visited categories, LDA defining venues, heatmap of Weekday/hour activity. You can switch between the clusters and see how they differ. The second aspect is made available by two main components: chord diagram of transitions between categories and a map, where the transitions between particular venues of selected categories pairs are displayed. With this you can see, what are the top-10 categories where people from a certain cluster go after they go to the Gym, what those places are, how they are distributed across the city and even the whole world.
